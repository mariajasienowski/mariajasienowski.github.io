About Me

I am a native from Venezuela and I’ve been an educator in New York City Public Schools for 13 years. I teach all levels of Spanish language including AP Language and Literature. I have a bachelor's degree in Spanish Literature from Queens College, a Masters Degree in Adolescent Education from Mercy College and I am currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Spanish Language and Culture at Salamanca University in Spain.

Living in New York City for the past 27 years helped me understand the importance and the challenges of learning a new language. I use this experience to better understand my students’ need and provide them with different strategies that will help them acquire the Spanish language more efficiently.
